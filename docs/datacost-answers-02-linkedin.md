**DataCost Answers #2: Stolen Phone in South Africa — What to Do Immediately**

A stolen phone can quickly become a banking, SIM-swap and identity-security problem—especially if it was unlocked or the thief knows the screen PIN.

The new DataCost guide puts the urgent actions first: get to safety, protect the bank, lock the phone through Apple Find My or Google Find Hub, block the SIM or eSIM, request IMEI blacklisting, report the theft to SAPS, and secure important accounts.

It also explains the difference between SIM blocking and IMEI blacklisting, what to record before remote erasure, and how manufacturer tools from Samsung, Huawei and Xiaomi fit behind the primary Apple and Google recovery methods.
