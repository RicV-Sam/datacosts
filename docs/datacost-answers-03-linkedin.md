**DataCost Answers #3: Should I Buy Airtime or Data in South Africa?**

For most smartphone users, data is the better default because it is easier to budget. But buying a data bundle does not automatically protect your airtime.

If the bundle runs out or expires, out-of-bundle settings can matter. Subscriptions, premium services and other charges linked to the number also need to be checked separately.

The practical starting point:

- Buy data for WhatsApp, apps, browsing, banking, maps and streaming.
- Use airtime or a voice bundle for ordinary cellular calls and SMS.
- Consider an all-in-one bundle for regular mixed usage.
- For strict control, choose once-off data and block or cap out-of-bundle spending.
