# DataCost Answers workflow

DataCost Answers turns genuine South African mobile-consumer questions into researched, reusable help without creating thin pages or a separate blog system.

1. **Real question:** Capture the consumer's actual problem and remove names, account details, phone numbers and other private information.
2. **Research:** Verify the answer against current official network guidance and, where relevant, the exact device or service involved.
3. **Answer page or update:** Prefer improving the best existing guide. Create a new guide only when the question has a distinct intent and enough substance for a useful standalone answer. Use the `DataCostAnswerIntro` and `DataCostAnswerCta` components, assign the next series number, and keep the answer-first summary concise.
4. **Direct reply:** Answer the person directly, linking to the published guide when useful. Make clear that DataCost cannot access or change a private network account.
5. **LinkedIn post:** Draft a `DataCost Answers #N` company-page post with the problem, one or two practical takeaways, the guide URL and the question-submission CTA.
6. **FAQ where relevant:** Add a focused FAQ to the page only when it clarifies a recurring follow-up. Avoid duplicating the main answer or manufacturing variants.

Question submissions use `hello@datacost.co.za` through `/contact/#questions`.

The series should reinforce DataCost as an independent South African mobile consumer help resource. Do not identify the person who asked unless they have explicitly agreed, and do not promise publication or account-specific support.
